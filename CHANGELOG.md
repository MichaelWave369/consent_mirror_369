### Safety framing

ConsentMirror369 is an education and reflection project. It does not diagnose people, prove intent, replace therapy, replace legal advice, or guarantee safety.

A pattern is a cue to slow down, not proof that someone is malicious.

### Privacy framing

The reflection log uses browser local storage. Entries are not sent to a server by the app. Local storage is not encrypted, so anyone with access to the same browser profile may be able to view saved entries.
