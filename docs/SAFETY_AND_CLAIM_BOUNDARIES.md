# Safety and Claim Boundaries

ConsentMirror369 is an education and reflection project.

## It does not claim to

- diagnose abuse
- prove intent
- classify people as good or bad
- replace therapy, legal advice, medical advice, or crisis support
- guarantee safety

## It does claim to

- teach pattern literacy
- encourage pausing before pressured decisions
- support clear consent
- offer respectful boundary language
- encourage trusted support when needed

## Safety note

If there is immediate danger, threats, stalking, coercive control, self-harm risk, or fear for physical safety, do not rely on the app alone. Contact trusted support, local emergency services, or a qualified professional resource.
