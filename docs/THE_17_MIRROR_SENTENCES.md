# The 17 Mirror Sentences

These are recognition mirrors, not debate weapons.

| # | Phrase | Possible pattern | Mirror question |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Everyone knows... | Social pressure | Who exactly is everyone, and what evidence are they using? |
| 2 | You are smart, so you will agree... | Flattery pressure | Am I being respected, or praised into agreement? |
| 3 | After everything I have done for you... | Guilt pressure | Was there a clear agreement, or is obligation being created after the fact? |
| 4 | Do not overthink it. | Discernment pressure | Why is my need to think being discouraged? |
| 5 | You only have one chance. | Urgency pressure | What happens if I slow down? |
| 6 | Real friends or real family do this. | Identity pressure | Is my identity being honored, or used as a leash? |
| 7 | I am just asking questions. | Leading question fog | Are these questions opening clarity or steering me toward a preset answer? |
| 8 | You are too sensitive. | Emotional dismissal | Is my experience being understood, or erased? |
| 9 | Trust me. | Evidence bypass | What evidence, history, or accountability supports trust here? |
| 10 | If you loved me, you would... | Love as pressure | Is love being invited, or used as a test? |
| 11 | They do not want you to know... | Hidden enemy hook | Is this expanding my clarity, or narrowing my world? |
| 12 | You are either with us or against us. | False binary | What options are being hidden? |
| 13 | You owe me. | Obligation pressure | What was freely given, what was agreed, and what is now being demanded? |
| 14 | Only I understand you. | Isolation pressure | Is this connection supporting my wider life, or cutting me off from it? |
| 15 | Do not tell anyone. | Secrecy pressure | Would this situation survive gentle outside light? |
| 16 | You will regret this. | Fear pressure | Is this wisdom, or intimidation? |
| 17 | This is for your own good. | Care without consent | Do I still have choice? |
