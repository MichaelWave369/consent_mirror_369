# Scenario Examples

ConsentMirror369 includes real-world practice scenarios so users can learn through context, not only through isolated phrases.

## Current categories

- Family
- Faith
- Workplace
- Online
- Sales
- Relationship

## Scenario structure

Each scenario includes:

- `id`
- `category`
- `title`
- `situation`
- `sample_phrase`
- `possible_patterns`
- `mirror_questions`
- `gentle_response`

## Design principle

Scenarios should be gentle, non-accusing, and practical. They should help users pause and choose clearly without encouraging blame, escalation, or counter-control.

## Safety reminder

If a situation may involve danger, threats, coercive control, stalking, or retaliation risk, do not encourage direct confrontation. Prioritize safety, trusted support, and qualified help.
